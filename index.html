EVERGLOW

    Everglow is a South Korean girl group under Yuehua Entertainment. The group is composed of six members: E:U, Sihyeon, Mia, Onda, Aisha, and Yiren. Everglow debuted on March 18, 2019, with the single album Arrival of Everglow.

Members

ONDA (Jo Serim)

    Onda (온다) is a South Korean singer under Yue Hua Entertainment. She is a member of the girl group EVERGLOW. She is also known for being a former contestant on the competitive reality television show, Idol School.

MIA (Han Eunji)

    Mia (미아) is a South Korean singer under Yue Hua Entertainment. She is the main vocalist and main dancer of the girl group EVERGLOW.

E:U (Park Jiwon)

    E:U (이유) is a South Korean rapper, singer and songwriter under Yue Hua Entertainment. She is the main rapper and the main dancer of the girl group EVERGLOW.

SIHYEON (Kim Sihyeon)

    Sihyeon (시현) is a South Korean singer under Yue Hua Entertainment. She is the leader of the girl group EVERGLOW. She is also known for being a former contestant on the competitive reality television show, produce 101.

AISHA (Heo Yoorim)

    Aisha (아샤) is a South Korean singer and rapper under Yue Hua Entertainment. She is a member of the girl group EVERGLOW.

YIREN (Wang Yiren)

    Yiren (怡人/이런) is a Chinese singer and rapper under Yue hua Entertainment. She is a member and maknae of the girl group Everglow. She is also known for being a former contestant on the competitive reality television show, produce 101.
